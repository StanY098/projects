Other Camera Equipments

Except the three basic elements ( ISO, Aperture & Shutter), other external equipment may also bring you a great success on taking any photographs. In the following, I will introduce several equipment for you to try on: tripod, external flash light, external shutter cord and cleaning supply.

Tripod

Tripod is a stand with three legs. With the three legs, a camera can stand very firmly that it will not move. There are many situations that tripod is very useful for photographing, for example, group photos, night views, self-taking photos and so on. For most of the tripods, they have at least three sections of legs so that users can adjust the height according to users' needs. If it is not high enough even though the three sections of legs have been extended, the stick at the middle of the tripod can also allow you to make it much higher by pulling up the stick.

With more functions in a tripod, some of the tripods also the angle balancing sensor to detect if the camera (or the setting of the tripod) is balanced or not (such that if the liquid in the sensor is at the middle, the camera is placed completely horizontally.)

External Flash light

External flash light is a flash light not to be installed in camera. It gives a great help to give supplmentary light for photos. You can plug it on the top of camera or use some wireless equipment to connect both the flash light and camera together. Therefore, you will be able to fill more light for the necessary parts. Also, inside the flash light, you can pull out a paper or a plastic guard. They are very useful for spreading the light equally.

Some people may have questions about why we still need an external flash light when we have already had the internal flash light and why the professionals do not use the internal flash light but the external. It is because the internal flash light has the disadvantages of the following:

  • limited area to be lighted (which will also make light too concentrated)
  • immovable that you cannot choose the area you want to light on
  • fixed intensity (which may make the light too intense when we do not need that much)

External flash light can solve all problems I mentioned from internal flash light. External flash light is movable, has a paper or plastic guard to spread out lightt and adjust the intensity of light from the setting of external flash light. Therefore, external flash light brings great advantages while internal flash light cannot.

External Shutter Cord

External shutter cord is a cord that can connect to camera. With the external shutter cord, users can control the time use to take photographs manually. To make it work, we need to change the setting of aperture to "BULB". As I mentioned before, the range of aperture usually for a camera is from 1/4000 seconds to 30 seconds. "BULB" is right out of (or next to) the 30 seconds setting. Therefore, as we adjust to "BULB", we just need to adjust to 30 seconds and then the next one.

The external shutter cord is very useful for taking night view or star view (star or star track). If you love stars and love to take some star photographs, external shutter cord will be your great helper.

Cleaning Supply

Since camera lens are very important for photographing, it is needed to be careful to clean the lens. Otherwise, the whole lens will be useless if it is damaged. Therefore, a special cleaning supply for cameras is introduced for users. I will introduce the following three: air blower, cleaning fluid and microfiver cleaning cloths.

  • Air Blower

    Air Blower is like an air pump seen in a science lab. However, it has a totally different function. In order to clean out dust without having chances to damage the lens, the air blower can blow out the dust without any contact. Therefore, air blower is a very nice tool to clear out dust from the surface of the lens.

  • Cleaning Fluid

    Sometimes there are some oil or fingerprint stain. It is hard to clean only by water (hard to dry and hard to wash). Also, it may also leave some marks on lens when water is going to be dried out. The special cleaning fluid can help clean the stain directly and completely but we don't have to worry about drying out the fluid or water. As we use it with microfiber cleaning cloths, all stain can be removed immediately.

  • Microfiber Cleaning Cloths

    Normal cloth materials damage the lens very easily. A special and soft cleaning cloth is needed. Microfiber cleaning cloths is special designed cloths. Its materials are very capable to clean up the lens without bringing any damage. Therefore, to use cloth to clean lens, microfiber cleaning cloths is needed.
