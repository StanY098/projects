Purpose

Welcome to this photo beginner webpage! This webpage can help you understand the basic knowledge of photographing, especially for how you better deal with the functions in cameras. The primary functions in a camera are three: ISO, Aperture and Shutter. Also, this webpage will also introduce other general external components and a comparison on several beginner-level cameras. If you want more information about photographing, you can also sign up for receiving through your email. Have fun and enjoy learning!
