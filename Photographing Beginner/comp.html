Full Frame Cameras vs DSLR Cameras

The major difference between Full Frame cameras and DSLR cameras is the field of view. Suppose Full Frame cameras take pictures with field of 1. DSLR cameras have the field with around 0.85. Full Frame cameras are more expensive (as you can compare the pirce ranges in the table below). However, even though DSLR has the disadvantages of smaller view of pictures, the quality and image capacity needed to store is less than the pictures taken by Full Frame cameras. Therefore, both have their advantages and disadvantages. Choose according to your need.

Nikon D3300

Nikon D3300 is a DSLR type camera. It has a really good resolution (24.2MP) and is also economic for customers. In most websites recently, it is the no.1 camera for beginner. Its features can really improve the quality and feeling of taking photographs and easy to use. For beginners, it is like a dictionary for learners to begin. Its features are: a special step by step guide mode, like playing any phone or video games for the first time. Also its SD slot is in grip side, not the battery side that users are more convenient to change SD cards. The LCD Screen is improved to be more visible in Sunlight. Therefore, Nikon D3300 can be the first choice!

Cannon Rebel SL1

Cannon Rebel SL1 is followed by Nikon D3300. Cannon Rebel SL1 has resolution of 18MP. Its ISO range is 25,600. It is a DSLR type camera. Its special feature is like smartphones nowadays that it allows users to choose focus by touching screen, not only by some normal buttons to customize focus. Therefoer, Cannon Rebel SL1 can provide another way for users to make desired picture focus.

Cannon 1300D

Cannon 1300D is a DSLR type camera. It has 24MP resolution and 12,800 ISO range, the smallest range among these six cameras. Cannon 1300D is pretty economic, around $500. Cannon 1300D is special for built-in Wi-Fi and NFC to make it easy to connect to a smartphone and also the selection of in-camera creative filters.

Nikon D5500

Nikon D5500 is a DSLR type camera. It has 24.2MP resolution, providing a pretty good picture and view quality. Its ISO range is 25,600. Same as Cannon 1300D, the price range is around $500. Its feature is not so special, but it has touch screen feature.

Cannon EOS 6D

Cannon EOS 6D is a Full Frame type camera. It has resolution of 20MP and ISO range of 102,400. It has the biggest range among all these six cameras. Cannon EOS 6D is able to shoot in low light. Even in a low light (not enough light) situation, Cannon 6D can have a better performance on photographing. It also has a built-in GPS and WiFi that pictures can always be tracked and shared at any time! Therefore, Cannon 6D is a picture historian to record and to keep the best memory.

Nikon D750

Nikon D750 is a Full Frame type camera. Its resolution is 24MP with ISO range of 51,200. Nikon D750 has a vari-angle screen to adjust to the most comfortable angle to take pictures. It also brings convenience to choose options by touching screen. It has a high resolution sensor, to take a higher-quality picture. WiFi is built in in order to provide an efficient way to upload pictures as soon as possible and also share to friends. Therefore, Nikon D750 provides the best experiences to take eye-catching pictures and share to friends and family immediately.
